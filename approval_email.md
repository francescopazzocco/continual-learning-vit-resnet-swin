Me (Francesco Pazzocco) Alberto Casellato and Riccardo Corte would like to propose a self-defined project.

**Title:** ViT vs. ResNet under Continual Learning

We want to compare whether standard continual learning methods (EWC, Experience Replay) work the same way on Vision Transformers as they do on CNNs. Most CL research was done on ResNets, but ViTs have different architecture properties (global attention, different representation geometry) that could change how forgetting happens.

We'd train both a ResNet-18 and a ViT-Small from scratch on Split-CIFAR-100 with vanilla fine-tuning, EWC, and replay — pure PyTorch.

Let us know if that works or if you'd like any changes.
